Welcome to the new Signal protocol-based app that adds security and peace-of-mind to your digital communications...with a colorful twist.

⌄

How Does It Work?

Flowmoji is an app that is built on top of Signal Protocol, a method of end-to-end encryption for communications created by Open Whisper Systems. Signal keeps messages extra secure because it combines multiple strategies to ensure safety, including something called the "Double Ratchet Algorithm." This algorithm renews one of two keys - one for sending and one for receiving - every time a message is sent, advancing the ratchet and barring old keys from decrypting new messages.

What Does Flowmoji Add?

Flowmoji adds another layer of security using obfuscation and noise strategies. While Signal's encryption protocol is very safe, no encryption is completely secure. Everytime a user sends a text message, Flowmoji fires several messages comprised of emojis, meaning that if someone breaks encryption on a message, they will likely find cartoon faces instead of sensitive material. Flowmoji diminishes the rewards for the resources spent on trying to break encryption.
